Visit Toronto
Toronto is a vibrant city known for its iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and bustling arts scene.
Top three activities to do in Toronto

  • Visit CN Tower

    "Experience breathtaking views from one of the world's tallest towers. The CN Tower offers a glass floor, revolving restaurant, and edge-walk experience."

  • Visit Casa Loma

    "Explore Toronto's very own castle, complete with secret passageways, gardens, and an impressive view of the city skyline."

  • Visit Royal Ontario Museum

    "Dive into history and culture at the Royal Ontario Museum, featuring exhibits on natural history, world cultures, and art."
